About FrontMisbar

We help analysts explore Earth observation data quickly with a clean, map‑first interface.

Our Mission

FrontMisbar streamlines discovery and visualization of satellite layers like NDVI, moisture indices, and more. Draw an area, pick dates, and instantly overlay results on the map.

What’s Inside

  • Fast base map switching (OSM, Satellite, Dark, Light, Topographic, NDVI)
  • Simple date filtering and cloud percentage controls
  • Export imagery in PNG or TIFF
  • Statistics over time for selected regions

Tech

Built with Angular, Leaflet, and Angular Material.
